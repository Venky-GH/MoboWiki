Verdict

The LG company has claimed that the LG X Venture has passed 14 different MIL-STD 810G tests, including drop and temperature evaluations. Moreover, the smartphone has a long battery life and excellent cameras. The handset is perfectly designed for people who want a buddy for camping, hiking, climbing and other outdoor activities.
