Verdict

Like most other premium brands, HTC too is looking to expand its presence in the mid range smartphone market with the launch of HTC Desire 728 Ultra Edition. Although it has good amount of features compared to what other premium brands offer in this range, it still lack many specs like metallic body, fingerprint sensor, full HD display etc which are pretty standard for chinese phones in this price point. Still if you are unsure about quality of chinese smartphones and are looking for a branded option with good features at affordable price, this device is perfect for you.
