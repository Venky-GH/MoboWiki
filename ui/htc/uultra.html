Verdict

The HTC U Ultra is a very good choice for the user and has sound battery backup. The cameras capture awesome images and the phone also has ample storage available. You will enjoy watching videos and playing games in the screen as the display is very nice, thanks to higher pixel density and resolution. Performance wise, the superior RAM and processor together is ideal for multitasking and high end gaming, you can go for this phone.
