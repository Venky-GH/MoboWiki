Verdict

If you are on the lookout for a tablet that is very sleek and portable and also has a powerful configuration, then the Apple iPad Mini 4 WiFi 16GB is for you. It also supports Touch ID control which imparts better security over your device as well as online transactions. However the low amount of internal storage is a point of concern as the tablet doesn't come with an expansion slot.
